## Understanding Common Sports Injuries

Sports injuries typically result from overuse, impact, improper technique, or inadequate warm-ups. Some injuries happen suddenly (acute), while others build over time (chronic).

### Most Common Sports Injuries Include:

*   **ACL & Ligament Tears:** Usually caused by sudden stops or direction changes in sports like football or basketball.
*   **Shoulder Dislocations:** Common in weightlifting, contact sports, or falls.
*   **Meniscus Injuries:** Resulting from knee twists during running or squatting.
*   **Rotator Cuff Tears:** Often seen in athletes who do repeated overhead motions like swimming or tennis.
*   **Overuse Injuries:** Like tennis elbow, shin splints, or stress fractures caused by repetitive motion or insufficient recovery time.

## Why Do These Injuries Happen So Often?

There are several reasons sports injuries are so common:

*   Intense physical activity without proper conditioning
*   Skipping warm-ups and stretches
*   Incorrect posture or technique
*   Not allowing enough recovery time
*   Returning to sport too soon after a previous injury

The key is **prevention, early diagnosis, and expert-guided recovery.**
